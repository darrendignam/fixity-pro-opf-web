Data Integrity. Done. Simply.

You have digital files on your computer that you care about. A lot. You worry about how you would ever know if anything ever happened to them and you hope that nothing ever does. Fixity Pro turns worry into certainty and hope into the information you need.

Fixity Pro maintained by Open Preservation Foundation.

Open software and standards are key to long-term digital access. The OPF provides expertise and support, empowering the digital preservation community to develop sustainable resources.

AVP and the Open Preservation Foundation (OPF) are excited to announce that AVP has transferred ownership and intellectual property rights of Fixity Pro to OPF. This transfer represents our mutual commitment to ensuring the long-term sustainability and continued development of this essential digital preservation tool.

How will the transfer to OPF affect users?

  • Registration is no longer necessary and has been disabled.
  • The software is no longer subscription and is free to use for anyone
  • It is no longer necessary to obtain a trial or subscription license key
  • We are continuing to run the subscription validation service as the current software needs this to continue to function
